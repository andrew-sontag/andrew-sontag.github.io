TAing


I have worked as a teaching assistant (TA) for UPenn calculus classes since Fall 2021.


My responsibilities have included:

  • Giving twice-weekly lectures
  • Holding twice-weekly recitations
  • Holding weekly office hours
  • Holding additional exam review sessions
  • Proctoring weekly quizzes and final exam
  • Grading weekly quizzes and final exam

I have TAed the following classes:

  • Fall 2021: UPenn Math 1400 (single variable calculus)
  • Spring 2022: UPenn Math 1400 (single variable calculus)
  • Fall 2022: UPenn Math 1400 (single variable calculus)
  • Spring 2023: UPenn Math 1410 (multivariable calculus)
